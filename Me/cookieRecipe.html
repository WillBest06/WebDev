How to make cookies

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup (115g) butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup (100g) brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup (50g) granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 1/4 cups (155g) all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 3/4 cup (130g) chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Cream together butter, brown sugar, and granulated sugar until light and fluffy.
  3. Mix in vanilla and egg until smooth.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk flour, baking soda, and salt. Gradually add to wet ingredients.
  5. Fold in chocolate chips.
  6. Scoop dough onto baking sheet (about 1 tbsp each).
  7. Bake for 8–10 minutes until edges are golden.
  8. Let cool for a couple of minutes (if you can resist).
